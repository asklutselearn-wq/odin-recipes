Kelewele

A versatile spicy fried plantain dish which be enjoyed as a main dish, side or snack. Guaranteed enjoyment.

Ingredients

  • Ripe Plantain
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Spices
  • Groundnuts or Cashew nuts (optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare stew with fresh tomates, tomato puree, and seasoning.
  2. Add rice
  3. Leave to simmer for 20 minutes
  4. Serve
