Jollof

This meal is a savory mix of rice and tomato sauce which will definitely give you fond memories of home.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Fresh Tomatoes
  • Tomato Puree
  • Seasoning

Steps

  1. Prepare stew with fresh tomates, tomato puree, and seasoning.
  2. Add rice
  3. Leave to simmer for 20 minutes
  4. Serve
